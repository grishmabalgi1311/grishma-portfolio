About Me

I’m a mechatronics and robotics engineer focused on designing and developing robotic systems that combine strong mechanical foundations with intelligent embedded control. During my undergraduate years at VJTI, I mentored multiple engineering and robotics projects, contributing to mechanical design, automation, and hardware integration.

I gained hands-on industry experience as a Machine Learning Research Intern at Schneider Electric, working on digital twins, reduced-order models, fault detection, and embedded ML systems for industrial equipment. I have also held key leadership roles, including Vice Captain of VJTI Racing and Coordinator at the Training & Placement Office, where I managed teams, project timelines, and industry collaborations.

I’m currently pursuing my MSc in Mechatronics and Robotics at New York University, expanding my expertise in robotics, simulation, embedded systems, and automation.
